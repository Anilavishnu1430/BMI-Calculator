BMI Categories

Understand what your BMI result means

Underweight

BMI below 18.5

You may need to gain weight for better health.

Normal

BMI 18.5 – 24.9

Great! You are in the healthy weight range.

Overweight

BMI 25 – 29.9

Consider exercise and balanced nutrition.

Obese

BMI 30 or above

Consult a doctor and create a health plan.
